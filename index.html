about me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with experience mainly focusing on Front-End Development. For most of my life I had planned to be in the medical field, but after switching gears and falling in love with all things tech, I started Web Development as a freelance job. I am passionate about using my skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript to create beautiful websites that satisfy the needs of my client, and go above and beyond expectation. I sweat the details, and as a result driven person I want my work to reflect that.

I am happiest when I'm problem solving, exploring and making ideas come to life. Outside the computer, I am a servant to my many plants, a baker, and an avid learner. My love of learning languages applies not only to code, but also to those of the spoken variety!
